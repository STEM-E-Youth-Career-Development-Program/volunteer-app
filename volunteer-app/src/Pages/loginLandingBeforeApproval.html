Welcome to STEM-E

Thank you for choosing to volunteer with STEM-E. We’re excited to have you join our incredible community and can’t wait to onboard you.

Next Steps:

1. You will receive an email requesting that will allow you to book a slot for the orientation.
2. Once you book the slot, you will get a confirmation email with meeting details.
3. In the orientation, the “Volunteer Coordinators” will give you an introduction of what your journey STEM-E would look like.
4. During the same orientation meeting, the “Volunteer Coordinators” will approve your account post which you will be able to sign-in.
5. Once you’re able to sign-in, your first step would be to sign the “Internship Waiver form."
6. Post signing the internship waiver, you are eligible to start your internship by picking the roles you are interested in and we will also create a time sheet for you to clock your hours.
